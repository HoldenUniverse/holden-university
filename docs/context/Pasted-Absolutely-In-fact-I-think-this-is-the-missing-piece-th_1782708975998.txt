Implementation Across the Ecosystem

Every discipline follows the same six-stage learning model:

Phase 1 – Foundation

Students learn the science, terminology, equipment, standards, and theory.

Phase 2 – Laboratory

Hands-on practice with tools, instruments, and equipment.

Phase 3 – Digital Simulation

IntegrityOS and RiskGateIQ place students inside realistic industrial scenarios where they apply what they learned under pressure.

Phase 4 – Operational Integration

Students work across disciplines. An instrumentation student collaborates with inspection, electrical, process, coatings, and mechanical students to solve facility-wide problems.

Phase 5 – Operation Fieldproof

Students complete a capstone mission that combines technical execution, communication, documentation, safety, and decision-making in a simulated industrial environment.

Phase 6 – Workforce Deployment

Graduates receive a verified digital workforce profile through Deployd, with training records, simulation performance, competency evidence, and employer-ready documentation.
